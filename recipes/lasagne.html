Lasagne Recipe

Hugh Fearnley Whittingstall's Lasagne

The secret to a great lasagne is quality of the sauce, and the key to that is coarse ground beef and long, slow cooking. Serves six to eight.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 good knob of butter
  • 1 medium oniom, finely chopped
  • 1 stick celery, finely diced
  • 1 large carrot, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 125g streaky bacon, finely diced
  • 750g coarsely ground beef
  • 100g free-range chicken liver, chopped fine (optional)
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • 250ml dry whole milk
  • A couple of grating of nutmeg
  • 250ml dry white wine
  • 400g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 bay leaves
