Courgette Falafel Recipe

Courgette Falafel Recipe

Use up those courgettes in tasty succulent courgette falafel

Ingredients:

For the falafel:

  • 1 400g tin of cooked chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1 medium courgette, shredded
  • 7tbsp (chickpea) flour
  • 1tsp each salt, ground corriander & cumin
  • squeeze of lemon
  • oil for frying

For the dip:

  • 4tbsp vegan yoghurt
  • 1tbsp tahini
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2tsp each garlic powder & chilli flakes
  • squeeze of lemon

Method:

  1. Pulse chickpeas, onion, garlic and parsley.
  2. Combine with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Shape in to falafel, about 1tbsp per falafel.
  4. Fry until golden brown or bake at 200c/400f/gas mark 6 for 25 mins
  5. Combine dip ingredients and serve
