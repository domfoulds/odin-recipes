Chickpea Masala Recipe

So Vegan's Chickpea Masala Sandwich

Weâ€™ll find any excuse to add chickpeas to our favourite meals. Here we combine them with spices, yoghurt and mayo to create a truly epic sandwich.

  • You can make this gluten-free using gluten-free bread.
  • Feel free to add your own fillings! We usually add whatever salad ingredients we have lying around in the fridge.
  • You can make the chickpea mixture ahead of time and store it in an air-tight container for 2 days in the fridge.

Ingredients:

For the masala:

  • 1x400g tin of chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • salt & pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled & rinsed
  • 1 thumb of ginger, chopped
  • 1/2 red chilli, chopped
  • 1.5tsp cumin powder
  • 1tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup vegan plain yoghurt

To serve:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • mango chutney
  • baby spinach leaves
  • tomato, sliced
  • cucumber, sliced
  • red onion, sliced
  • 1 small handful of fresh coriander

Method:

  1. First add some olive oil to a pan. Next add the chickpeas along with a pinch of salt and pepper and fry for 8 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, red chilli, ground cumin, paprika and tomato puree to the pan and fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Transfer the chickpea mixture to a bowl and stir in the vegan mayonnaise and yoghurt.
  4. To serve, spread a little mango chutney over a slice of bread.
  5. Next add some spinach leaves followed by the chickpea masala mixture, then some slices of tomato, cucumber, red onion and finally a few coriander leaves.
  6. Close the sandwich with a second slice of bread. Repeat for the second sandwich.
